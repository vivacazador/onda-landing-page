Who We Are

At SeedSense, we believe in empowering seed companies with innovative tools to streamline their trial management and make data-driven decisions.

Our Mission

SeedSense exists to revolutionize seed trials by providing cutting-edge software that simplifies complex processes, reduces human error, and delivers actionable insights. We are driven to help seed companies bring the best varieties to market faster and more efficiently.

Our Values

  • Innovation: Continuously evolving to meet the needs of modern agriculture.
  • Collaboration: Building strong partnerships with seed companies and industry leaders.
  • Sustainability: Promoting practices that protect and preserve our environment.

Meet Our Team

Our team of agricultural experts, software engineers, and data scientists are dedicated to delivering the best tools for seed companies. We are passionate about innovation and committed to excellence.
