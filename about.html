About Us

Two builders.
One mission.

We started Onda because we saw businesses drowning in spreadsheets and workarounds. We knew we could build something better.

Our Story

Onda Software Solutions was founded by two friends from California Polytechnic in San Luis Obispo. Drawing on a shared background in farming and computer science, Joao and Hunter set out to address niche software problems that larger companies overlook — starting with agriculture and expanding from there.

We believe the next decade will belong to businesses that embrace technology on their own terms. Our job is to build the tools that make that possible — practical, opinionated software that fits how your team actually works, not how a generic platform assumes you do.

The Team

Joao Cavalcanti
Co-Founder · Engineering

Born in Espírito Santo, Brazil, Joao initially studied aeronautical engineering before graduating at the top of his class in Computer Science at Cal Poly. He spent years building web applications for one of the world's largest edtech companies. Outside the office, he's an endurance athlete — a retired Brazilian jiu-jitsu competitor who now runs marathons along the West Coast.

🌾
Hunter Singh
Co-Founder · Product

Hunter grew up on a farm on California's Central Coast, spending summers on tractors and running a goat-grazing business during college. After graduating, he worked in product development for a California seed dealer and took a gap year in Mexico — surfing and building a restaurant that supports a local children's home. A self-taught programmer, he's driven to bring real technology to industries still running on paper.
