Our Story

Onda Software Solutions was founded by two California Polytechnic graduates Hunter Singh and Joao Cavalcanti. Using their mutual knowledge of farming and computer science, the pair focus on addressing niche problems in the agricultural industry.

CoFounders Joao and Hunter

Joao Cavalcanti was born and raised in Espirito Santo, Brazil. He graduated top of his class in Computer Sciences and has spent years developing web applications for the worlds largest education technology company.

Hunter Singh is from California's Imperial Valley. Son of an agriculutre family, he has started a few agriculutre based businesses. He has industry experice in livestock mangament and most recently worked in the California vegetable seed industry.
