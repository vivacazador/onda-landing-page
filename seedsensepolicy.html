SeedSense Privacy Policy

Current Effective Date: November 19, 2024
This Privacy Policy describes how Onda Software Solutions LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects the data of users ("you" or "your") when using the SeedSense software application ("SeedSense" or "the App"). By using SeedSense, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.
01

Data Collection

SeedSense collects data inputted by users, including but not limited to company information, trial data and notes, user activity, and location data.

02

Data Usage

The data collected is used internally at Onda Software Solutions LLC for purposes including but not limited to training internal models, data analysis, and improving the functionality of the SeedSense application.

03

Data Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties or other clients. Your data remains private and is used solely for internal purposes.

04

Data Storage

All collected data is stored on cloud servers with standard security measures in place to protect user data.

05

User Consent and Data Usage

By using SeedSense, enterprise customers agree that data generated through the use of SeedSense will be collected and utilized internally by Onda Software Solutions LLC. Customers can contact us to request the deletion of their data at any time.

06

Data Retention

Data is stored indefinitely unless a deletion request is made by the customer.

07

User Data Access and Updates

Users can update their data by adjusting it within the SeedSense platform, which will update the data we collect.

08

Security Measures

We implement standard security protocols to protect your data, including secure cloud storage.

09

Third-Party Services

We do not share data with third parties, with the exception of our cloud storage provider.

10

Age Restrictions

SeedSense is limited to users 18 years and older.

11

Device Permissions

SeedSense may request device permissions such as access to your camera, location, and contacts. Users have the option to refuse GPS access on their devices.

12

Policy Updates

Any updates to this Privacy Policy will be communicated to enterprise users via email.
